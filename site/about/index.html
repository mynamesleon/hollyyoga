About Holly

As a passionate believer in the power of intuition and compassion, I teach an intuition-based approach to yoga and meditation. I focus on helping people find their own way in their body, by moving with awareness and ease within their yoga practice.

I first came across yoga as a child when I attended a class run by a family friend with my mother. I cherished the peace this centering practice gave me and I loved how alive I felt! Through my teens I developed a committed home practice and took every opportunity that came my way to learn more.

I am a registered and fully insured yoga teacher. I graduated from the Sivananda Yoga Centre London with a RYT 200 hour Yoga Alliance International certificate. This was an enlightening experience and I will always be grateful for all that I learnt from the Swamiâ€™s and fellow students alike.

I am also a Strala Yoga Guide, having completed the Strala Intensive course with Tara Stiles in London.

Over the years, I have met several inspirational yoga teachers who taught me how to find my own yoga teacher groove and set me on the road to finding my own teaching style, which is evolving day by day.

My goal is to share a style of yoga that is fun, laid-back and intuitive.

Whilst training as a Dive Master in Croatia, I met my husband, who not only shares my passion for the sea, but is also one of my biggest supporters and inspiration. We currently live in London, and spend each summer with friends and family in the Adriatic Sea.

Another influential figure on my journey has been my mother, Lisa Sture, who is a well-known British Raw Food advocate and Nutritionist. She has not only taught me how to best nourish my body, but also how to nourish my dreams.
