FAQs

What style of yoga do you teach?

I primarily teach a grounded style of flow yoga, moving freely to cultivate strength, balance, and awareness. As a passionate believer in the power of intuition and compassion, my teaching style focuses on helping people find their own way in their body, by moving with awareness and ease within their yoga practice.

Do I need to be young and super flexible to practice yoga?

Absolutely not! Yoga is primarily concerned with lovingly moving towards being the best version of you - be that physically or spiritually. It has nothing to do with being able to touch your toes! So follow your intuition and have some fun!

Do I need my own yoga mat?

Yes, please bring your own mat. It is always more hygienic, and having your own mat at home will encourage you to continue your personal practice outside of class. Decent yoga mats can be purchased relatively cheaply nowadays.
